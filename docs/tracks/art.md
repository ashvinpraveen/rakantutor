# Generated Art Track: Biodiversity & Cultural Heritage of Malaysia

### Theme Overview: Visualising Malaysia’s Biodiversity & Culture
The Generated Art track explores the intersection of Malaysian culture, nature, and artificial intelligence. Participants use generative tools to create stunning visual narratives that highlight the importance of preserving our natural biodiversity and cultural heritage.

### Mission
Create a series of AI-generated artworks that tell a story about Malaysia’s rich natural environment or its unique cultural traditions. Use AI as a collaborator to push the boundaries of digital aesthetics.

### Masterclass Information: Creative AI Artistry
During the masterclass, you will:
1. **Prompt Engineering:** Master advanced techniques for Midjourney, DALL·E, and Stable Diffusion.
2. **Artistic Direction:** Learn how to maintain stylistic consistency across a series.
3. **Ethics and Ownership:** Discuss the role of the artist in the age of AI.

### Step-by-Step Guide
1. **Concept Development:** Choose a specific aspect of Malaysian nature or culture to explore.
2. **Iterative Generation:** Explore hundreds of prompts to find the right "feel" and aesthetic.
3. **Curation:** Select the most impactful images that tell a cohesive story.
4. **Post-Processing:** Use traditional digital tools to refine and polish the AI outputs.
5. **Artist Statement:** Write a narrative explaining the creative choice and the AI tools used.

### Deliverables
- **Art Portfolio:** 3-5 high-resolution AI-generated images.
- **Process Document:** A breakdown of the prompts, tools, and iterations used.
- **Artist Statement:** 300 words explaining the story behind the art.

### Judging Criteria
| Criterion | Weight | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Creativity** | 40% | Originality of the concept and the visual impact of the artwork. |
| **Design Process** | 30% | Depth of exploration through prompt refinement and iteration. |
| **Storytelling** | 30% | How well the artwork connects to the theme and conveys a message. |
