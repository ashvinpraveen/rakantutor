# Innovation Track: AI-Native EdTech Startups

### Theme Overview: Building the Future of Education
The Innovation Track is for the builders, the dreamers, and the future founders. We believe that students are the true experts on the problems within the education system. Whether it’s the crushing weight of teacher admin, the boredom of repetitive revision, or the lack of personalized guidance—you know these pain points better than anyone. 

This year, we’re challenging you to build **AI-native EdTech startups** that solve these problems for yourself and your peers. This isn’t just about a project; it’s about creating something genuinely useful that people actually use.

### Mission
Develop a functional AI-powered product and get real traction. Your goal is to launch your startup, onboard active users, and prove that your solution has the potential to go viral and impact the Malaysian education landscape at a policy level.

### Masterclass Information: Vibe Coding & Startup Hustle
During the masterclass, you will:
1. **The Expert Lens:** Learn how to turn your everyday frustrations as a student into a startup product-market fit.
2. **Vibe Coding & Rapid Prototyping:** Watch live demos of "vibe coding"—using AI to build functional, high-quality apps in hours, not months.
3. **Growth Hacking & Traction:** Strategies to get your first 100, 500, or 1,000 users using TikTok and social sharing.
4. **Pitching to Policy Makers:** How to present your utility in a way that captures the attention of the Ministry of Education (MOE).

### Sub-Themes & Ideas
*   **Gamify Everything:** Turn boring subjects into addictive social learning games.
*   **Teacher Superpowers:** Use AI to slash admin burdens and help teachers focus on teaching.
*   **Personal Learning Co-pilots:** Tools that work with your specific school notes and materials.
*   **AI Teachers & Voice Learning:** Multi-modal education using natural language processing (powered by tools like SeaVoice).
*   **The "Free University":** Open-access, high-quality learning platforms for everyone.

### Step-by-Step Guide
1. **Identify Your Own Pain:** What is the most annoying thing about your school day? Solve that.
2. **Vibe Code the MVP:** Use AI to build a Minimum Viable Product that actually works.
3. **The Launch:** Get it into the hands of your friends, classmates, and teachers.
4. **Iterate with Traction:** Use feedback and usage data (e.g., "10,000 quizzes created") to improve.
5. **The Startup Showcase:** Prepare to "live demo" your product to your peers and judges.

### Deliverables
- **The Live Product:** A working link to your AI-native startup.
- **Traction Report:** Evidence of usage (active users, total interactions, or viral reach).
- **Startup Pitch Deck:** A compelling narrative on the problem, solution, and expansion roadmap.

### Judging Criteria
| Criterion | Weight | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Utility & Problem Fit** | 30% | How genuinely useful is this to students/teachers? Does it solve a real pain? |
| **Product Quality (The 'Wow' Factor)** | 30% | The quality of the prototype and the innovative use of AI-native features. |
| **Traction & Momentum** | 40% | Real-world engagement. How many people used it? Does it have viral potential? |
