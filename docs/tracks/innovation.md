# Innovation Track: AI for Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

### Theme Overview: AI-driven Solutions for Sustainable Cities & Communities
The Innovation Track invites visionaries to design disruptive AI solutions that tackle major societal challenges. This year, we focus on the **United Nations SDGs**, particularly Sustainable Cities and Communities (SDG 11). Participants focus on the conceptual design, scalability, and social impact of their ideas.

### Mission
Design an AI-driven product or service that addresses a significant social or environmental problem. No coding is required; the focus is on the business case, user experience, and measurable impact.

### Masterclass Information: Design Thinking & Social Impact
During the masterclass, you will:
1. **Human-Centered Design:** Learn how to identify user needs and pain points.
2. **Business Case Development:** Structure a proposal for scalability and sustainability.
3. **Impact Measurement:** Learn how to quantify the social or environmental benefits of your solution.

### Step-by-Step Guide
1. **Identify a Gap:** Find a significant problem in urban living or community wellbeing.
2. **AI Solutioning:** Conceptualize how AI can uniquely solve this problem.
3. **User Experience Design:** Create mockups or wireframes of the solution.
4. **Impact Analysis:** Estimate the scale of the positive change your idea could create.
5. **Pitch Preparation:** Prepare a compelling narrative that connects the problem to the solution.

### Deliverables
- **Innovation Proposal:** A 10-page pitch deck or report.
- **Product Mockup:** Visual representations of the product (app screens, website wireframes).
- **Impact Roadmap:** A plan showing how the solution will be deployed and scaled.

### Judging Criteria
| Criterion | Weight | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Problem Statement** | 40% | Depth of understanding of the social challenge and its importance. |
| **Feasibility** | 30% | Realism of the solution and its potential for real-world scaling. |
| **Innovation** | 30% | Uniqueness of the AI application and the creative "wow" factor. |
