# Engineering Track: Smart City Initiatives

### Theme Overview: Smart City Initiatives
In this year’s Engineering Track of the National AI Competition, participants will dive into the exciting world of **Smart City Initiatives**—where artificial intelligence and engineering shape the future of urban living. A smart city uses technology to improve daily life by addressing challenges such as traffic congestion, energy consumption, waste management, and safety.

### Mission
Design AI-powered engineering solutions that make cities more efficient, sustainable, and people-friendly. Projects may use data, sensors, automation, or machine learning to solve real-world urban challenges in Malaysia.

### Masterclass Information: Prototype Workshop
During the masterclass, you will:
1. **Understand prototyping:** Why it matters and how to test ideas quickly.
2. **CAD for the prototype:** Translate concepts into CAD models for clear visualization.
3. **Prototype with a purpose:** Build a proof-of-concept prototype demonstrating your solution in a smart city context.

### Step-by-Step Guide
1. **Problem Identification:** Observe real-world challenges (e.g., traffic, pollution).
2. **Research and Context Understanding:** Study existing solutions and data sources.
3. **System Design:** Break the system into inputs, processing (AI), and outputs.
4. **Solution Evaluation and Feasibility:** Assess technical feasibility and impact.
5. **Prototype Development and Testing:** Build a physical model or software simulation.
6. **Proposal Preparation:** Compile everything into a clear report.

### Brainstorming Techniques
- **Mind Mapping:** Explore causes, stakeholders, and AI opportunities.
- **User Journey Mapping:** Understand pain points from a user perspective.
- **Problem Prioritisation:** Select the most impactful and feasible problem.

### Deliverables
- **Preliminary:** Proposal Report (Problem statement, Proposed product design).
- **Final:** Prototype, Testing/Evaluation of prototype, Poster.

### Judging Criteria
| Criterion | Weight | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Problem Understanding** | 30% | Clarity of the defined problem and its relevance to Malaysian urban life. |
| **AI-Enabled Solution** | 40% | Quality of the solution and the effectiveness of AI integration. |
| **Prototype & Impact** | 30% | Demonstration of the core idea and potential real-world impact. |
