# GenAI Track: Visit Malaysia 2026 - Digital Storytelling

### Theme Overview: Showcasing Malaysia Through AI-Generated Visuals
The GenAI Track challenges students to blend cultural heritage with cutting-edge technology. Using AI-generated images and creative storytelling, participants will create engaging, TikTok-style video narratives that promote Malaysia as a premier travel destination for 2026.

### Mission
Produce a high-impact, short-form video (TikTok/Reel style) that uses a series of AI-generated stills and a custom voice-over to tell a compelling story about Malaysia’s beauty, culture, or hidden gems.

### Masterclass Information: Storytelling with AI Visuals
During the masterclass, you will:
1. **Advanced Image Generation:** Master the art of generating high-quality, consistent stills that capture the "Visit Malaysia 2026" vibe.
2. **AI Voice-Over & Scripting:** Learn how to write engaging scripts and use AI voice tools to add a professional narrative layer.
3. **Short-Form Video Editing:** Techniques for compiling stills into dynamic, TikTok-style content with smooth transitions and viral appeal.

### Step-by-Step Guide
1. **Develop a Concept:** Choose a theme related to "Visit Malaysia 2026" (e.g., food, nature, architecture, or city life).
2. **Storyboard with AI:** Generate 10-15 high-quality AI stills that follow a narrative arc.
3. **Scripting & Voice-Over:** Write a 45-60 second script and generate a voice-over that fits the mood.
4. **Compile & Edit:** Use video editing tools to sync the visuals with the voice-over, adding captions and music.
5. **Final Polish:** Ensure the video feels "TikTok-native" – fast-paced, engaging, and vertical.

### Deliverables
- **TikTok-Style Video:** A 45-60 second vertical video (MP4 format).
- **Prompt Log:** A list of the AI prompts used to generate the visuals.
- **Story Statement:** A 200-word explanation of the creative vision and the choice of narrator/tone.

### Judging Criteria
| Criterion | Weight | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Visual Creativity** | 40% | The quality, consistency, and aesthetic appeal of the AI-generated stills. |
| **Storytelling & Narrative** | 40% | Compelling use of script and voice-over to promote the Visit Malaysia 2026 theme. |
| **Technical Execution** | 20% | Quality of video editing, pacing, and overall "viral" potential. |
